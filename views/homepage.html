About

Fundi is an online web app which makes collaboration between students easier. Create and join groups in any field of study. Take your studies to the next level by chatting with team mates and sharing links which benefit the whole group. Find groups based on your interests. Set up meetings online or face to face while adhering to COVID-19 policies. Tired of trying to figure out whose home is most central for the meeting? We have you covered. We believe in democracy, so we even let you vote on all group decisions.
